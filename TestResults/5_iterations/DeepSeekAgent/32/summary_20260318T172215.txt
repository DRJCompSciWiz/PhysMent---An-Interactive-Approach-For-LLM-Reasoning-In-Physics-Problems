### Theoretical Analysis:
1. **Forces**:
   - Wall (frictionless): Normal force \(N_w\) perpendicular to wall (horizontal)
   - Ground: Normal force \(N_g\) upward, friction force \(f\) horizontal
   - Gravity: \(mg\) downward at center of mass

2. **Equilibrium Conditions**:
   - **ΣF_x = 0**: \(N_w - f = 0\) → \(f = N_w\)
   - **ΣF_y = 0**: \(N_g - mg = 0\) → \(N_g = mg\)

     N_w \cdot L \sin\theta = mg \cdot \frac{L}{2} \cos\theta
     \]
     \[
     N_w = \frac{mg \cos\theta}{2 \sin\theta}
     \]

   \[
   f \leq \mu N_g \implies \frac{mg \cos\theta}{2 \sin\theta} \leq \mu mg
   \]
   \[
   \mu \geq \frac{1}{2 \tan\theta}
   \]
