For an Atwood machine with:
- Heavy block: M = 5.0 kg
- Light block: m = 2.0 kg

The theoretical acceleration is:
a = (M - m)g / (M + m) = (5-2)×9.81 / (5+2) = 3×9.81/7 = 4.204 m/s²

The tension in the rope is:
T = m(g + a) = 2 × (9.81 + 4.204) = 28.03 N

W = mg = 2 × 9.81 = 19.62 N

T/W_light = 28.03/19.62 = 1.43
